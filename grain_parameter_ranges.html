Validation Rules

  • mu_r > 0.12: Warning - Grain becomes excessive
  • sigma_r > 0.05: Warning - Extreme clumping artifacts
  • sigma_r/mu_r > 1.0: Warning - Physically unrealistic clumping
  • filter_sigma < 0.6: Warning - Shows individual grains (only for extreme zoom)
  • filter_sigma > 1.0: Warning - Over-smoothed, loses texture
